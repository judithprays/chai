About

i was putting together a site designed by kevin byrd, a button i was placing was pixel perfect and yet looked off. a man of pixel perfection, i asked what i should do and he cited the parthenon effect - stating above all he wants it to look right. i spent the rest of the day thinking about the parameters with which we design. on this same night, i was reading the torah blog my mom and i do together. my mom's contributes by synthesizing the portion to 180 words, or 360 words if that's too tight. i always wondered about her choice of this number, about the tendency of jews in general, to use this number, and i wondered -

what happens when 18 is chosen at every clear opportunity as the design parameter?
